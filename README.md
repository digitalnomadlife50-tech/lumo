# lumo.

> AI gives you speed. lumo gives you clarity.

A decision-making and communication tool for IC product managers. Walk through a structured 6-step flow, and Lumo produces tailored messages for engineering, design, and leadership.

**[Try it live → trylumo.co](https://trylumo.co)**

---

### what it does

1. paste your messy situation
2. lumo reads it back structured
3. see your options and their costs
4. commit to a direction
5. get three audience-tailored messages you can copy and send

### the problem it solves

AI gave PMs speed. What it didn't give them is clarity on the judgment calls, or the seven different ways you need to communicate one decision to seven different people. Lumo handles both.

### stack

- **AI**: Claude (Anthropic) for situation analysis and message generation
